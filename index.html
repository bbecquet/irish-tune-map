Find tunes of Irish traditional music by the place they refer to in their title.

Example: if you click on Galway, you'll find tunes like:

  • All the way to Galway
  • The Maids Of Galway
  • Roving Galway Boy
  • etc.

Links go to the tune page on The Session, a wonderful database of Irish traditional music by Jeremy Keith, which is also the datasource for this map.

Please note:

  • Most traditional pieces of music have a lot of alternative names. I've considered all of them when matching with places, as well as the place names in Irish Gaelic when available.
  • I've ignored place names which are also common names, but there are still some false matchings.
